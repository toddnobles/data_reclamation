DEPARTMENT OF THE INTERIOR
UNITED STATES INDIAN SERVICE

Colville Agency, Nespelem, Wash.,
June 17, 1919.

Superintendent E. H. Hammond,
Cushman Indian School,
Tacoma, Wash.

Dear Mr. Hammond:

In accordance with my telegram of even date viz;

"Parents request and I recommend Agnes and Nancy Parsons and Josie Freidlander be permitted to return during vacation period. I am mailing checks to cover transportation. Parents will meet stage Wednesday. Please wire if children can leave tomorrow."

I enclose herewith check #3009 for $25 payable to your order against the account of Nancy Parsons. Check #3008 for $25 payable to your order, against the account of Agnes Parsons. Check #3010 for $15.16 against the account of Josie Freidlander payable to your order.

The last named check for $15.16 is the balance to the credit of Josie Freidlander. Mr. Parsons the guardian of this child will arrange to send you $10 additional to cover return expenses when school re-opens in September.

I enclose for your files vacation requests signed by Mr. A. B. Parsons for these three pupils.

Yours very truly,

Fred C. Morgan
Superintendent
Vacation Request For Pupil.

Nespelem, Wash.,
June 16, 1919.

To: Supt., Cushman Indian School,
Tacoma, Washington.

Dear Sir:

I, the undersigned, respectfully request that leave of absence be granted Josie Friedlander from June 19, 1919, to Sept. 5, 1919, to enable her to visit her home. I agree to pay all expenses in connection therewith, including return fare, and that this pupil will be returned to school on or before the last day of the leave of absence. In case she is not returned to school the money deposited with you to cover her return fare shall be forfeited.

A. E. Parsons
Guardian

Cushman Indian School,
Tacoma, Wash. Dec. 16, 1918.

Supt. Fred C. Morgan,
Colville Agency,
Raspelem, Wash.

Dear Mr. Morgan:

I have your letter of December 10th. Please advise Mrs. Chuchuwaix, the grandmother of George Louie that her grandson is well and getting along nicely in school. The Flu left Cushman long ago and there is nothing to worry about.

Harry Sorimpt is still in the hospital recovering from the attack of influenza and pneumonia. He is in no immediate danger, is only kept at the hospital to grow strong, and to protect him from exposure and a relapse.

Hattie Friedlander is still in the hospital, but improving every day.

Nancy Moses has been in the hospital a few days with a headache; and Annie Bourgesu has the rheumatism; all the other Colville children are well. If any inquiries are made by parents, please advise them.

Very respectfully,

Superintendent

May 19...1920

Name of Student... George or Oyt. Louie (Oushman)

Parent or Guardian... Chuchuwax, Gr. No.

Reservation... Colville, Wash.

Tuition for the year ending... 5/44/20 $200

Superintendent's Certificate.

1. Certificate of Inability to Pay.

I hereby certify that neither George or Oyt. Louie nor his parents have any funds or income out of which they are financially able to pay the above bill or any part thereof.

2. Certificate of Partial Ability to Pay.

I hereby certify that... or his parents can meet the above bill in part, and that $... is considered to be a fair amount for them to contribute to the education of this student.

Supt Colville Indian Agency Nespelem, Wash. Superintendent.

Superintendents are requested to assist this office in making collection of the amount of tuition due, when circumstances justify its payment. If parents are able to pay a part of the amount, please fill out Certificate 2, and arrange for the collection of the amount recommended. If parents or pupils are unable to meet this expense, Certificate 1 should be filled. Please return this statement with the proper notation.

Very respectfully,

Superintendent.
